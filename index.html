Dr. Craig Wright

The creator of BitCoin, aka. "Satoshi Nakamoto."

Here's a timeline of Dr. Craig Wright's life:

  • October 1970 - Born in Brisbane, Australia
  • 1987 - Graduated high school from Padua College in Brisbane, Australia
  • 2003 - Doctor of Theology - United Theological College
  • 2005 - Master of Networking and Systems Administration - Charles Sturt University
  • 2006 - Master of Management (Information Technology) - Charles Sturt University
  • 2008 - Masters of Laws with Commendation in International Commercial Law - University of Northumbria
  • October 31st, 2008 - Dr. Wright published an academic paper under the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto: Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System
  • January 3rd, 2009 - The BitCoin network came into existence with Satoshi Nakamoto mining the genesis block of bitcoin
  • 2009 - Master of Statistics - University of Newcastle
